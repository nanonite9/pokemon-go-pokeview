About Us

We are based off Niantic Inc's game, Pokémon Go. Our mission
is to provide users with reliable information on Pokéstops
and Gyms worldwide to bring Trainers together and be more
successful with group raids.

We encourage users to link their neighbourhood or corresponding
Discord links if applicable to help visiting/new Trainers to
the area.

The rating can be used to indicate the potential number
of Pokémon that may spawn during active Lures, or the
number of active users that frequent the area around
the Pokéstop/Gym. Trainers can also look to coordinate
Raid Hours accordingly.

Our rating scale consists of the berries used in
Pokémon Go! Take a look at the corresponding meanings below:
